    eThesis Repository Library

    Explore, Access, Thrive – Your Gateway to Academic Excellence

    The eThesis Repository Library is an online platform designed for students of the College of Computing and Information Sciences to effortlessly access, search, and explore a vast collection of academic theses. Whether you're conducting research, reviewing past projects, or seeking inspiration, this repository offers seamless access to archived theses, helping you stay informed, enhance your knowledge, and achieve your academic goals.
