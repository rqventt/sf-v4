    About the Scholar Finds

    The Scholar Finds brings valuable improvements to the thesis process, benefiting students, faculty, and administrators alike. By promoting efficiency, security, organization, and user-centric design, it empowers academic institutions to manage thesis submissions more effectively while providing a smoother, more productive experience for all users.

    A Quick Start with Scholar Finds

    Get started with Scholar Finds in this brief video guide! Learn to search, save resources, create citations, and organize research efficiently. Perfect for students and researchers looking to streamline their academic work.

    OUR VISION


    Scholar Finds envisions to provide a centralized platform that offers quick and easy access to a vast collection of theses and abstracts, striving to make research more accessible and less burdensome for the CCIS students.

    OUR MISSION


    To optimize the thesis management and enhance the research experience of the CCIS students at the University of Makati by providing a centralized digital platform that will make finding theses and abstracts effortless, allowing students to save time and effort.

    Developer Team's Journey

    From the College of Innovative Teacher Education-Higher School ng UMak (CITE-HSU), the Group 3 of G12-03 CPG, from the Academic Year 2024-2025, developed Scholar Finds as part of their requirements for Computer Programming 4, Work Immersion, and Research Project. This platform is designed to simplify the research process and enhance convenience for students and researchers in their respective fields.
